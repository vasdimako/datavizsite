About me

My name is Vasilis Dimakopoulos. I'm 26 and I'm in the DSIT Biomedical Data Science specialization. My main interests are neurobiology, algorithms and explainable science. I hope to pursue a PhD in data science applications to neurobiology.

My background

I completed an undergraduate degree at the University of York in Psychology with a focus on Neuroscience and continued on to a Computational Neuroscience MSc at the University of Nottingham.

During my studies I developed an interest in programming and its applications in science, both for biological systems modelling and more practical tasks, such as data analysis and visualization.
